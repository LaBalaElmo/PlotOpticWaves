Angles of the modes

In this page you can find the angles for different modes, for this 4 fields must be filled, each field is a value that must be known, the first is 2a field, it is the diameter of the core and the value must be in micrometers (µm), the next field is lambda field (λ), it is the wavelength and this value must be in nanometers (nm) and finally the other 2 field are the refractive indices (n1, n2), these are just numeric values.
